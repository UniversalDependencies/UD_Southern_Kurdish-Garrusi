shortdef: 'advmod with modal modality'

The _advmod:mmod_ relation is used for adverbal modal modifiers.

~~~ sdparse
Паряк, Митя вачкодизе сонзэ. \n Maybe, Mitya hit him.
advmod:mmod(вачкодизе, Паряк)

~~~
