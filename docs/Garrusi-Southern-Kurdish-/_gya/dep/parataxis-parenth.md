The `parataxis:parenth` relation is used for parenthetical clauses.

~~~ sdparse
ʔá̰-nɛ̀ [mɛ̀ tɛ́ káì] gbó hɛ̌n zǎŋ kòlèɲáà \n now [come on, damn it] he's coming to Kolegnan
parataxis:parenth(gbó, tɛ́)
~~~
