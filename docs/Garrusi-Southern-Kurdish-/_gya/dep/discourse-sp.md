Gbaya has a system of enunciative particles, which are always placed at the end of a clause and serve an enunciative function by specifying the speaker’s point of view.
They are tagged as `PART` and annotated with the relation `discourse:sp` (sp for “sentence particle”).

~~~ sdparse
Wàntò péí ɗòŋ wó \\ Wanto comes back
discourse:sp (péí, wó)
~~~
