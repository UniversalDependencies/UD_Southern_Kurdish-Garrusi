title: 'obl:float'
shortdef: 'oblique as a floating modifier'

---

The relation `obl:float` is used for floating modifiers _увесь_ 'all', _кожны_ 'every', _адзін_ 'only; alone', _абодва_ 'both', _сам_ 'oneself' etc. that modify objective, dative, etc. argument in the clause.   
`obl:float` can attach directly to the verb (or other predicative) if the (semantically modified) argument is ommited.

~~~ sdparse
Ён рассыпаўся ўвесь . \n He fell apart all over . 
obl:float(рассыпаўся, ўвесь) 
obl:float(fell, all)
~~~

~~~ sdparse
А цяпер праверце самі, дзе нараджальнасьць большая . \n Now check for yourself where the birth rate is higher . 
obl:float(праверце, самі) 
obl:float(check, yourself)
~~~
