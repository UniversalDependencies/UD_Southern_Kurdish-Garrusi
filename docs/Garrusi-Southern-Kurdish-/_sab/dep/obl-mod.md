The `obl:mod` relation for nominal adjuncts.

Here is an example from **UD_Bokota-ChibErgIS**:

~~~ sdparse
ba be hoke Rio Luis . \n You will go to Rio Luis.
obl:mod (hoke, Rio)
~~~
