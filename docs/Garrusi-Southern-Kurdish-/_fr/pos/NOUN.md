### Definition

Nouns are a part of speech typically denoting a person, place, thing, animal or idea. Common nouns are tagged as `NOUN`.

### Examples

- _fille_ "girl"
- _chat_ "cat"
- _arbre_ "tree"
- _air_ "air"
- _beauté_ "beauty"
