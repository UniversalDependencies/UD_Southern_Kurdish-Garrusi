`Ergative` refers to a auxiliary marker, _te_, that signalizes a change in the alignment type. 

### <a name="Yes">`Yes`</a>: Yes

#### Examples

* _te <b>te</b> 're ĩ'ahöri _&nbsp; “He used to hit me.”
