The `Encl` feature applies to all parts of speech.

### <a name="C">`C`</a>: the enclitic -ც (too, even)

#### Examples

* _იმასა<b>ც</b>_ 'also him/her'

### <a name="Ve">`Ve`</a>: the enclitic -ვე (also, too; the same, the very)

#### Examples

* _დღეს<b>ვე</b>_ 'already today'
