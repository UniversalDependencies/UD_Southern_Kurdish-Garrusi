The dependent of an `advcl:purp` relation is a purposive adverbial clause.

Here is an example from **UD_Ika-ChibErgIS**:

~~~ sdparse
pera ʉnkʉtakʉn zoyana \n He went picking pears.
advcl:purp (zoyana, ʉnkʉtakʉn)
~~~
