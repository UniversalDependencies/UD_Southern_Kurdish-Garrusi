### Definition

A subordinating conjunction is a conjunction that links constructions by making one of them a constituent of the other. The subordinating
conjunction typically marks the incorporated constituent which has the status of a (subordinate) clause.

For _coordinating conjunctions_ see [CCONJ](_oge/pos/CCONJ).
