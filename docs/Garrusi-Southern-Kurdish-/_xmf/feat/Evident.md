The `Evident` feature applies to [NOUN](_xmf/pos/NOUN), [PROPN](_xmf/pos/PROPN), [ADJ](_xmf/pos/ADJ), [PRON](_xmf/pos/PRON), [NUM](_xmf/pos/NUM), [ADV](_xmf/pos/ADV) and [VERB](_xmf/pos/VERB). 

### <a name="Fh">`Fh`</a>: firsthand

#### Examples

* _გჷმიჩამჷ<b>-მაქი</b> (ɡəmit͡ʃɑmə<b>-mɑki</b>)_ 'it is for sale as I said' etc.

### <a name="Nfh">`Nfh`</a>: non-firsthand

#### Examples

* _მოლმოჩქუ<b>შო</b> (mɔlmɔt͡ʃku<b>ʃɔ</b>)_ 'he/she/it sent it to me as smb. said' etc.
