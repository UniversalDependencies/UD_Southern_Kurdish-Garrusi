### Definition

Megrelian particles, can be represented by a suffix attached to an inflected word (e.g. _<b>ვა</b>ჭარა (<b>vɑ</b>t͡ʃʼɑrɑ)_ 'do not write' etc.), or by an independent word (e.g. _ხოლო (xɔlɔ)_ 'too'). Particles are subdivided into the following lexical classes: interrogative (PartType=Int), affirmative (PartType=Ta), negative (PartType=Neg), modal (PartType=Tm) and focus (PartType=Foc).

### Corresponding language-specific part-of-speech tags

Part:	Particle

- Q (interrogative particles):      PartType=Int
- Ta (affirmative particles):			  PartType=Ta
- Inf (infinitive particles):			  PartType=Inf
- Neg (negative particles):				  PartType=Neg
- Int, Proh (emphasis particles):		PartType=Emp
- Foc (focus particles):            PartType=Foc

## Examples
infinitive
- interrogative particles: _-ო (-ɔ)_ '?' etc.
- affirmative particles: _ქო- (kɔ-)_ 'yes' etc.
- infinitive particles: _-მე (mɛ)_ 'any' etc.
- negative particles: _ვა (va)_ 'not' etc.
- emphasis particles: _ხოლო (xɔlɔ)_ 'too' etc.
- focus particles: _-ც (-t͡s)_ 'and' etc.
