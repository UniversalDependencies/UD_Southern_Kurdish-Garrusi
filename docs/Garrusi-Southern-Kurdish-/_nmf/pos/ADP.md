### Definition

`Adposition` is a cover term for prepositions and postpositions. In Naga-Suansu, some adpositions can also function as verbal particles.


### Examples

* _Tye puirawi bava chaha <b>maramda</b> traie_ "The woman cried **about** her loss"

* _Tye neo fencedi ra <b>zwehnn</b>e_ "The boy jumped **over** the fence"
