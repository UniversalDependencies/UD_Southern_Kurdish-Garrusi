title: 'advmod:tmod'
shortdef: 'temporal adverbial modifier'
udver: '2'
---

This semantical subrelation is used to single out those adverbial modifiers that express a temporal reference.

~~~ sdparse
andò vi poi lo Vas d' elezione 
advmod:tmod(andò, poi)
~~~
