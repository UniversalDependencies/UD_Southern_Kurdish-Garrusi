The `mark:rel` relation is used for the particle `ᡞ᠋` /`i` between an adjectival or nominal modifier and a nominal head.

~~~sdparse
ᠠᡞᠰᡞᠨ ᡤᡠ ᡞ ᡤᡞᠰᡠᠨ
compound(ᠠᡞᠰᡞᠨ︐ ᡤᡠ)
mark:rel(ᠠᡞᠰᡞᠨ︐ ᡞ᠋)
nmod(ᡤᡞᠰᡠᠨ︐ ᠠᡞᠰᡞᠨ)
~~~
