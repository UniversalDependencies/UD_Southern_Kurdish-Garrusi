title: 'obj:appl'
shortdef: 'applied direct object'
udver: '2'
---

Valency of a verb can be increased by an applicative suffix _-ba_.  A new object is introduced with a semantic role that would be otherwise coded as an oblique dependent. If the verb was intransitive, the new object will have the `obj:appl` relation.

~~~ sdparse
Tita ge ǁnā khoena sîsenba tama hâ . \n I DECL that people work_for not PFV .
obj:appl(sîsenba, khoena)
obj:appl(work_for, people)
~~
