The `vocative` relation is used to mark a dialogue participant addressed in a text. The relation links the addressee’s name to its host sentence. In Khoekhoe, the addressee usually appears with the vocative [case](naq-feat/Case) suffix _-e_.

~~~ sdparse
ǃGâi ǁgoas , ǃgôahesatse . \n Good morning , sir.VOC .
vocative(ǁgoas, ǃgôahesats-.e)
vocative(morning, sir.VOC)
~~~
