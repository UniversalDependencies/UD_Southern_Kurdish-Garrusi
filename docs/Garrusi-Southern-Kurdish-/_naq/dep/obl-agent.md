title: 'obl:agent'

The relation `obl:agent` is used for agents of passive verbs. Agents of passive verbs are nominals followed by [postposition](naq-pos/ADP) _xa_ "by".

~~~ sdparse
Petrub ge Marias xa ge mîǁamhe . \n Petru DECL Maria by PST be_offended .
nsubj:pass(mîǁamhe, Petrub)
obl:agend(mîǁamhe, Marias)
case(Marias, xa)
nsubj:pass(be_offended, Petru)
obl:agent(be_offended, Maria)
case(Maria, by)
~~~
