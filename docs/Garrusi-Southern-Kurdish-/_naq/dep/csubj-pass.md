A clausal passive subject is a clausal syntactic subject of a passive clause.

~~~ sdparse
ǁîb nî ǁōb ǀkha ǁkharahe ǃkhais ge noxoba mîǁguihe tama hâ . \n He will death with be_punished COMPL DECL still be_decided not PFV
csubj:pass(mîǁguihe, ǁkharahe)
csubj:pass(be_decided, be_punished)
~~~
