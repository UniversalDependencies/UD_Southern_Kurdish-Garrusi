This is a special class of adverbial modifiers. While other adverbial modifiers usually modify verbs, adjectives or adverbs, these emphasizers often modify noun phrases, including prepositional phrases. Used for adverbs such as _tsîn(a)_ "also" (and fixed multiword expression _=s tsîn(a)_)

~~~ sdparse
Sago tsîn ge ǃHūbaib ai ǀkhai . \n You also DECL Earth on not_exist .
advmod:emph(Sago, tsîn)
advmod:emph(You, also)
~~~

~~~ sdparse
ǁîs ǃnâ s tsîna ta ge ǀgôaba ūhâ . \n She in NMLZ also I DECL child have .
advmod:emph(ǁîs, s)
fixed(s, tsîna)
advmod:emph(She, NMLZ)
fixed(NMLZ, also)
~~~
