We use `reparandum` to indicate disfluencies overridden in a speech repair. The disfluency is the dependent of the repair. In spoken language, false starts have the `reparandum` relation.

~~~ sdparse
ǁGûgu ge ǁnāpa ǂganaga ... ǁgûgu ge sinki ai ǂnôa . \n Teeth DECL there inside ... teeth DECL sink in sit .
reparandum(ǂnôa, ǂganaga)
reparandum(sit, inside)
~~~

~~~ sdparse
Want [ n ] ǀnîsi ǀgaub ge . \n Because [ n ] maybe way DECL .
reparandum(ǀnîsi, n-3)
reparandum(maybe, n-12)
~~~
