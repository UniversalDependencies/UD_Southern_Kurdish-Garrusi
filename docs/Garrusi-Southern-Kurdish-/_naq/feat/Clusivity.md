`Clusivity` is a feature of the first-person plural personal [pronouns](naq-pos/PRON).

### <a name="In">`In`</a>: inclusive

Includes the addressee, i.e. we = I + you (+ optionally they).

#### Examples

* _sakhom_ "we two (masc. excl.)", _sam_ "we two (fem./neut. excl.)", _sage_ "we (masc. incl.)", _sase_ "we (fem. incl.)", _sada_ "we (neut. incl.)"

### <a name="Ex">`Ex`</a>: exclusive

Excludes the addressee, i.e. we = I + he/she/they.

#### Examples

* _sikhom_ "we two (masc. excl.)", _sim_ "we two (fem./neut. excl.)", _sige_ "we (masc. excl.)", _sise_ "we (fem. excl.)", _sida_ "we (neut. excl.)"
