In Khoekhoe, `Degree` is the feature of [nouns](naq-pos/NOUN), [adjectives](naq-pos/ADJ), and [verbs](naq-pos/VERB). The only value is diminutive.


### <a name="Dim">`Dim`</a>: diminutive

Morphologically derived form that indicates small size, or, metaphorically, affection towards the entity described by the noun, adjective, or a verb.

#### Examples

* _ǃās_ "settlement", _ǃāros_ "small/cute settlement"

* _ǂkhari_ "small", _ǂkhariro_ "tiny"

* _ǂoa_ "go out", _ǂoaro_ "go out for a little"
